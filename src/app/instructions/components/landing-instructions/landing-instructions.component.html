How to Play Splashy Canvas

Welcome to Splashy Canvas!

In this game, you'll be presented with an image divided into sections. Your task is to paint each section of the image.

  1. Select a color from the color picker located at the top of the screen.
  2. Touch a section of the image to paint it with the selected color.
  3. Continue selecting colors and painting sections until the entire image is colored.
  4. Have fun and be creative with your color choices!

Enjoy the game and let your creativity flow!
